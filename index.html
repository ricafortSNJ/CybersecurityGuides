Why You Should Study Cyber Security?

"It's not worth studying cybersecurity, as it's just a passing trend that will soon be replaced by something else."
Cybersecurity is a rapidly growing field, with a high demand for skilled professionals. Cybersecurity Ventures predicts that there will be 3.5 million unfilled cybersecurity jobs globally by 2021, and this number is expected to continue to grow in the coming years.
Cybersecurity professionals play a crucial role in protecting critical infrastructure such as power grids, water systems, and financial systems from cyberattacks. As the world becomes increasingly dependent on technology, the need to protect these systems becomes more important.

Is Cybersecurity Hard to Learn?

"Cybersecurity is just for "smart" people."
Cybersecurity can be a complex and challenging field to learn, as it involves many different technologies, concepts, and best practices. It also requires a deep understanding of the constantly evolving threat landscape, and the ability to stay current with new developments in the field.
That being said, with the growing need for cybersecurity professionals, many resources such as online courses, tutorials, and certifications are available to help individuals learn about cybersecurity. And with determination and effort, anyone can learn and succeed in cybersecurity.
